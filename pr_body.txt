💡 **What:** Removed the redundant `load_dotenv` call from `LLMClient.__init__`. Unused imports (`dotenv.load_dotenv`, `pathlib.Path`, and `re`) were also removed.
🎯 **Why:** The application already loads the `.env` file at its entry points (`run.py` and `scripts/smoke_openrouter.py`). Calling `load_dotenv` on every instantiation of `LLMClient` introduces unnecessary blocking filesystem I/O.
📊 **Measured Improvement:** In a microbenchmark instantiating `LLMClient` 1000 times, the time dropped from `37.2525` seconds to `33.7243` seconds. This represents an approximate 9.5% improvement in instantiation time.
